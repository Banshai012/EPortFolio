Sport & Other Activities

Table Tennis

For me, table tennis is much more than a pastime; it is a discipline that sharpens both the body and the mind. Often described as 'playing chess while running a 100-meter dash,' this sport has taught me the value of split-second decision-making and adaptability.
Every match challenges me to analyze my opponent’s strategy, anticipate their next move, and adjust my game plan instantly. This constant demand for focus and agility is something I apply to my professional life: staying calm under pressure and always looking for the most effective solution.

Gaming

For me, gaming is fundamentally a social and collaborative experience. Participating in multiplayer environments has taught me the importance of clear communication, leadership, and coordinating with diverse team members to achieve a common goal.
I enjoy the dynamic of stepping into a specific role within a team, trusting others to do their part, and adapting our strategy in real-time. It is a modern way to develop the soft skills essential for effective teamwork.
